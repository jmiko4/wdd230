Overview

Purpose

Let’s say you’re a photographer and you are looking for work; You need a website to display an attractive portfolio that showcases your best work for possible clients to view. This portfolio would also ideally have more information about you and how to contact you. My idea is to build a portfolio website for a photographer like me to showcase my best work and to attract more clients. This website will have a main page that leads to several different pages that showcase my different styles of photography and two additional pages that give more information about me and my contact information.

Audience

The audience of this website is possible clients interested in hiring me as a photographer or videographer. The audience may also be friends and family who want to see my images or work. The porfilio pages will display my best work for clients in a pleasing way.
