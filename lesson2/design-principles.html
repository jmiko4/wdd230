Design Principles Document

Justin Mikolajcik

White Space

Harvard Art Museum

https://harvardartmuseums.org/

This website has good use of white space between elements so the display does not feel cramped or cluttered. It makes it easy to understand page content and navigation is self explanatory.

Contrast

Huge

https://www.hugeinc.com/

Huge uses the dark pink background with white text to catch the users attention. This combined with the simple design makes the website very appealing and the information easily read.

Fitt's Law

Mid City Nursery

https://midcitynursery.com/

This website employs Fitt's law by putting a big button in the middle/bottom portion of the screen that says 'our location'. The location of the button makes it easy for users to press it without moving their thumb very far
